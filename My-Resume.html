My Resume

Research Interests 

Computational physics, data mining, cosmology, gravitational-wave astrophysics, general relativity, particle physics, climate change

Research Experience

GW Analysis in Deci-hertz Domain with Dr Apratim Ganguly, IUCAA Pune and Dr Shabnam Iyyani, IISER Thiruvananthapuram
Will begin in Jan 2023
I will be working with Dr Ganguly, Dr Iyyani and Kanchan Soni to explore the underlying population distribution of compact binary parameters in the decihertz band. This will involve predictive analysis, which will be important for future observatories like DECIGO. I will be undertaking this to fulfill the requirement for my minor degree in data science.  

Tests of General Relativity with Dr Frank Ohme, Albert Einstein Institute Hannover
July 2022 - Sept 2022
In this remote internhip, I worked in collaboration with Dr Krishnendu Nair from Dr Ohme's group to conduct tests of general relativity using gravitational-wave observations from binary mergers. We analysed the effect of mass and distance on the SNR of inspiralling binaries and on the mass of the graviton.

Higher-Order Modes in GW Analysis with Prof. Sanjit Mitra, IUCAA Pune
Apr 2021 - June 2021
I worked with Kanchan Soni from Prof. Mitra's research group to study the inclusion of higher-order modes in gravitational-wave analysis to improve the estimation of parameters. I learned to use the bilby_pipe pipeline and the Vim editor in this internship.

Reading Project with Dr Mamta Gulati, Thapar University Patiala
July 2020 - Sept 2020
Under Dr Gulati's guidance, I studied introductory concepts of astronomy using the books  Fundamental Astronomy by Hannu Karttunen and Astrophysics for Physicists by Arnab Rai Choudhari.

Projects and Workshops

A Star is [Un]Born: Understanding Stellar Collapse
I worked with Shubham Chury and Aadi Chaturvedi in this semester project for General Relativity and Cosmology in semester 7. We conducted a review of the metrics and the Tollmann-Oppenheimer-Volkhoff (TOV) equations of different stellar bodies. You can find our report here. 

Interferometer Noise in LIGO
In this project under the supervision of Dr Joy Mitra, IISER Thiruvananthapuram, Rtihika Ganesan and I conducted a literature survey to analyse the sources of thermal noise in LIGO interferometers and the feasibility for its reduction by change in mirror coatings. 

SCUDEM 2020
SCUDEM is an international mathematical modelling challenge organised by SIMIODE, a non-profit community of practice focused on a modeling first approach to teaching differential equations. Under the supervision of Dr Zehor Belkhatir, University of Leicestor England, I worked with Rithika Ganesan and Balaram Vishnu S to model the decay of oil agglomerates from the Deepwater Horizon Accident using differential equations. 

LISC Continuous Gravitational Waves Workshop
I participated in this online workshop organised by ICTS Bangalore in collaboration with LIGO India to learn the basics of CGW data analysis. This included matched filtering, F-statistic and parameter space metric. I also learned to use PyFstat to analyse CGW data. 

LIGO Gravitational Waves Science Communication Workshop 
This workshop was organised by IUCAA Pune and IISER Pune in collaboration with LIGO India where I participated in interactive sessions and assignments on effective science communication within and without academia.
